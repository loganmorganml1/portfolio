About Me

Problem solver and avid learner

Hello and welcome to my site! When I put my mind to something I dedicate myself to making the best possible product it can be. Whether it be a comic, print, or website (like this one!) I will do everything in my power to learn, create, and adapt until happy with the results.
I love to learn new things and look at a variety of mediums to help drive my creativity. When I'm not working on design you can find me working on my comic, walking my dog, or unwinding with a good book!
