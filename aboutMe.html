About Me

Problem solver and avid learner

Hello and welcome to my site! I am currently an analyst/graphic designer at VillageMD who works with day to day design requests, project management, and content development. I have an extensive background in design and have worked in a variety of design fields including consulting, advertisement, and museum exhibit/installation design. As of recent, I’ve taken an interest in web design and coding.

I love to learn new things and look at a variety of mediums to help drive my creativity, from commercial design to comics to installation art. Given the constraints on a given project, I think it’s important to look at different types of art to help see design in a new perspective. Because of this, I am an avid museum goer, and always dedicate some time when traveling to see the local art scene.
